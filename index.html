Privacy Policy for Deal Delights Page Posting

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

Welcome to Deal Delights Page Posting (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of our Facebook automation service.

1. Information We Collect

Our App is designed to automate posting on your Facebook Page. We prioritize your privacy and minimize data collection:

  • Facebook Access Token: We securely store the access token necessary to publish content to your Facebook Page. This token is encrypted and used solely for the purpose of automated posting as scheduled by you.
  • Content You Provide: We process the product pictures, captions, and affiliate links that you configure for the App to post. We do not use this content for any other purpose.
  • Technical Data: Our servers may automatically log standard technical information such as your IP address, request times, and status of posting activities for operational maintenance and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To automatically publish posts to your Facebook Page according to your predefined schedule (9 AM, 1 PM, 7 PM).
  • To maintain the service and ensure its security and functionality.
  • To provide customer support if needed.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. This does not include:

  • Facebook Platform: Content (images, captions, links) is shared with Facebook as per the app's core functionality to create posts on your Page.
  • Trusted Service Providers: We may use third-party hosting services to operate the App. These providers are obligated to keep your information confidential.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information where required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your Facebook access token is stored using encryption, and access to the backend systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access and Correction: You can review and update your posting schedule and content at any time within the App.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the App's access to your Facebook Page at any time through your Facebook settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your stored data by contacting us. Please note that revoking Facebook access also stops data collection.

6. Third-Party Links

The posts created by this App may include affiliate links. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
